Program Timeline

Join us at Korbhauser Weg 1, 28279 Bremen, Germany for an evening of tradition, food, and music.

Time Activity Details
14:30 - 15:00 Guest Arrival Traditional welcome with Ugadi Pachadi.
15:00 - 15:45 Inauguration & Prayer Jyothi Prajwalana & Panchanga Sravanam.
15:45 - 17:30 Cultural Extravaganza Kids performances, Classical dance, and Skits.
17:30 - 18:15 Adult performances & Awards Singing performances and recognition of volunteers.
18:15 - 19:30 🍛 Grand Ugadi Feast Traditional Vindu Bhojanam served to all guests.
19:30 - 20:30 🎧 DJ Dance Floor Non-stop Telugu & Bollywood hits to celebrate the New Year.
20:30 - 21:00 Closing & Cleaning Final photos, vote of thanks, and community cleanup.

⚠️ Note for Volunteers: Please report to the venue by 14:00 for decoration and setup assistance.
