About TAB

The Telugu Association Bremen (TAB) is dedicated to preserving our rich culture while building a strong network for Telugu people in Northern Germany. We bridge the gap between our roots and our current homes through celebration and support.

History of Excellence

Our community has grown stronger through shared memories. Our previous events like Sankranthi 2023 and Ugadi 2024 saw record-breaking attendance, and we continue that legacy this year.
