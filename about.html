Our Roots & Vision

The Telugu Association Bremen (TAB) is a community organization dedicated to preserving the rich heritage, language, and traditions of the Telugu-speaking diaspora in Northern Germany.

Whether you are a student at the University of Bremen, Hochschule Bremen, a professional in the Technology Park, or a family residing in the Weser region, TAB provides a "home away from home" to connect and celebrate.

The Spirit of Ugadi

Ugadi marks the beginning of the Prabava Nama Samvatsaram.

The Ugadi Pachadi is a unique dish with six tastes representing the six emotions of life: Sadness, Happiness, Anger, Fear, Disgust, and Surprise.
