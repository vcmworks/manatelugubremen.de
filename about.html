Our Roots & Vision

The Telugu Association Bremen (TAB) is a non-profit community organization dedicated to preserving the rich heritage, language, and traditions of the Telugu-speaking diaspora in Northern Germany.

Whether you are a student at the University of Bremen, a professional in the Technology Park, or a family residing in the Weser region, TAB provides a "home away from home" to connect and celebrate.

The Spirit of Ugadi

Ugadi marks the beginning of the Prabava Nama Samvatsaram. Central to the festival is the Ugadi Pachadi, a dish with six tastes representing the six emotions of life. It reminds us to face the coming year with equanimity.

A Legacy of Success

Our community has grown significantly over the years. We fondly look back at the massive success of our previous celebrations:

  • Sankranthi 2023: Our first major post-pandemic gathering.
  • Ugadi 2023: A vibrant showcase of local talent.
  • Sankranthi 2024: A sold-out event with traditional flavors.
